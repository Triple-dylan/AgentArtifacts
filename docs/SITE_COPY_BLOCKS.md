## Store FAQ (Home and Detail)
1. **What do I get after purchase?**  
Digital files (Markdown/JSON/YAML/code) delivered through the success/download page, including manifests and docs listed on each product page.
2. **Can I use these commercially?**  
Yes, under the selected license tier. Redistribution/resale of raw assets is not permitted unless explicitly stated.
3. **How do updates work?**  
One-time purchases include the current version. Optional updates feed/subscription grants ongoing update access.
4. **Do these work with my stack?**  
Each product lists compatibility badges (OpenAI, Anthropic, Cursor, LangChain, etc.) and dependencies.
5. **Are trading products investment advice?**  
No. Trading/prediction assets are tooling/education only. Users control execution and accept applicable disclosures.
6. **What is your refund policy?**  
Refunds are handled per the digital goods policy and misuse/fraud checks defined in Terms.
